ARDUINO JOYSTICK PROJECT

Arduino joystick is nothing but a variable resistor with angular movement. This way we can record the x axis and y axis of the joystick. This project was inspired from a nostalgic memory of me, playing spiderman game with a red color joystick.

ARDUINO Diabetes foot monitor

Nodemcu based diabetes foot monitor is a foot displacement measuring device for patients with diabetes neuropathy. Due to this condition people tend to develop ulceration under the foot which might lead to amputation of that foot if not treated on time. Unfortunately the p[atient will not feel any pain under the foot which makes it much more dangerous. I solved this problem by placing force sensitive resistors under the foot to measure the displaement. The difference in the displacement of the numb foot and normal foot will alert the patient for treatment. Force sensitive resistors are nothing but sensors whose resistance changes when a strain is applied on them.
