Bayesian Based Health Care App

Bayesian based health care app The app was developed to reduce the travel to visit hospitals for minor consultations. This app uses bayesian probability formula to predict the future values of the parameters. Talking about parameters they the day today activities like performing physical exercises or smoking or drinking etc., monitoring them and converting the observations as empirical data( probability of consitency of performing the exercise). This data is distributed over dependent parameters like cholesterol or blood glucose level which may or may not change over time. This app is completely developed using python.
